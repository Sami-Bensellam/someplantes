About us

SOME-PLANTES is a family team advocated to essential oils and their virtue. Its main concern is to produce essential oils in a scientific way and with the most efficient method using plants chosen from the best sites of Могоссо.

SOME-PLANTES produces its own essential oils and sells it directly to users to avoid middlemen. Its essential oils are produced under rigorous and systematic inspection. The essential oil's quality is tested with high performed techniques such as Gas chromatography coupled to mass spectra (GC-MS). Our customers can receive the chromatogram of each product if desired (The whole spectrum with chromatogram, Certificates of Analysis and MSDS are available upon request).
